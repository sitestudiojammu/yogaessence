Meet Palvi Gulati

Palvi Gulati (E-RYT 500)

8+ years · Ashtanga, Prenatal & Postnatal specialist

I am Palvi Gulati, a certified Ashtanga, prenatal, and postnatal yoga teacher based in Jammu. I have been running my own yoga studio for the past eight years, helping people improve their physical health, mental well-being, and overall lifestyle through yoga. I specialize in supporting women during pregnancy and postnatal recovery with safe and effective practices, while also guiding students of all levels to build strength, flexibility, and mindfulness in their daily lives. 🧘‍♀️🌿
